Chris Rawles

Staff Research Engineer · Google DeepMind

I work on LLM agents that operate computers. I'm currently on the Gemini Thinking & Reasoning team, focused on coding agents. Previously I led the AndroidWorld and Android in the Wild projects — large-scale benchmarks and datasets for computer use agents (CUAs).

Before research I was a software engineer at Google and Pivotal. I hold a B.A. from UC Berkeley.
